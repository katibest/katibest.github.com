Imagine That!

An app

The Urban Legend Institute (or ULI for short), is the foremost institution for exploration, investigation, and advancement of urban-legendary-type matters. And it happens to be the social enterprise attached to WordPlay.

I created a poster series to get the word out about ULI! Using the idea of explorations and investigation I explored and investigated antique stores to find the most interesting toys and objects. then used photos of them to make the posters an interactive search and find.
