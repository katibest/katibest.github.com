Imagine That!

App

An app that goes beyond the screen!

Technology is wonderful. This generation is all about and kids especially love their electronic devices, but maybe they love them a little too much. Modern technology is definitely beneficial, however, too much “tech time” can have a negative effect. Childhood is such a vital time to grow and develop. Imaginative and hands-on activities may seem like just playtime, but actually this type of play help develop life-long skills. Neither type of interaction is superior, so what kids is balance!!

imagine that, a transitional app, does just that! This app creates a balance for kids between the use of technology and the physical world around them.
